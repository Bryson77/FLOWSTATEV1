1. Overview

FlowState respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Pomodoro productivity app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email, display name, and authentication details.
  • Session data: Focus session durations, streaks, tasks completed, and dashboard metrics.
  • Spotify integration: If you connect Spotify, we access only the data required to play music; your password is never stored.
  • Device and usage data: Basic analytics to improve app functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain your account and app features.
  • Display session and streak data on your dashboard.
  • Enable Spotify music playback during focus sessions.
  • Communicate with you about account or service-related matters.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required to provide services (e.g., Spotify API). We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data.

5. Cookies and Analytics

FlowState may use cookies or local storage for functionality (like session persistence) and basic analytics to improve the user experience. No personal data is sold or shared.

6. Your Choices

You can manage your account information, disconnect Spotify at any time, and opt out of non-essential communications.

7. Children’s Privacy

FlowState is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of FlowState after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
